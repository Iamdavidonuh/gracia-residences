About Us

Gracia Residences offers comprehensive short to mid-term rental housing solutions, specializing in corporate accommodations for executives, nurses, and family getaways. Our dedicated team of experts is committed to delivering the exceptional customer service you deserve.

We streamline the process by signing multi-unit long-term leases, which saves you time and money by consolidating payments from multiple tenants. Partnering with residential, commercial landlords, and property managers seeking guaranteed income for their properties is our forte.

Recognizing that your property is a significant investment, we prioritize its care as much as you do. Each unit undergoes meticulous and regular deep cleaning to ensure utmost comfort and satisfaction for our guests at Gracia Residences

Wear & Tear

Our dedicated cleaning staff ensures your property is maintained to the highest standards, with frequently scheduled cleaning services. By keeping the property in A+ condition daily for our guests, we help prevent costly repairs.

No more Tenant Turnover

Securing new tenants for the space can swiftly become costly once the current tenant departs. Common issues include:

  1. Extensive cleaning and repainting.
  2. Listing fees or realtor commissions.
  3. Lengthy vacancies spanning multiple months.

Time To Sell

We understand Your property is an investment and when its time to sell we work with your schedule for showings and ensure that your property is in A+ condition always ready to show to potential buyers making it appealing and never worry about eviction moratoriums or worry about tenants vacating property on closing.

Stress free Guaranteed Rental Income

We assure you consistent income throughout the lease term, eliminating worries about tenants losing their jobs or timely payments. Our stress-free guarantee ensures you receive rent every month without fail

Have multiple units? No worries!

We execute long-term leases for multiple units simultaneously, streamlining your operations and saving you the time and expense of sourcing individual tenants for each unit. This approach simplifies rent collection and reduces monthly expenses.
